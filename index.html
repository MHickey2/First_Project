Welcome to my Website


Life can be busy, whether you are a Student, a Worker, a stay at home Mum, it can be hard to get the balance right between work and play. How do we turn off and experience life without worrying about our next crisis. It's important to step back and evaluate what we are doing right and what could be better. This website attempts to offer ways that you can try new practises that give you a chance to stop and breathe. They may inspire you to find your own coping strategies, but you never know what can make the difference, just open your mind and step into a new way of living, it will make living a whole lot easier for yourself and the people around you.


A holistic approach has been determined to be a valid way to promote health and wellbeing and can indeed have a positive affect in all areas of your life. So within this website, there are a selection of suggestions on how you can integrate some simple techniques that will go a long way to improving your life balance.

Body

Your body involves all aspects of your physical self. It includes your diet, your sleep, your exercise and how you treat your body in general. What do you do to maintain it in good shape. Do you live a sedentary lifestyle or are you always on the go? Do you have a good diet or do you overindulge on fastfood? It all has a bearing on your health. Could you do more to help your body? Our body Section will give you a brief introduction to ways you can start to make better lifestyle choices.

Mind

Your mind is a powerful tool, when it works well you can achieve almost anything, but it can also be detrimental to our wellbeing. Do you overthink about every little aspect of your life. Is stress beating you down and does it make everything so much worse. Or maybe you push yourself to the limit, and beyond your physical and mental capability. Could you do more to help your mind? Our Mind Section will provide you with ways to quiten down and use your mind more affectively.

Spirit

Not so obvious but just as important. A healthy spirit/soul can help you experience the joy in life. Do you feel worn down by life and stuck in a rut. Do you have a purpose or do you feel devoid of emotions. This can affect how you interact with others and can make it hard to feel positive when life gets you down. Could you be doing more to help your spirit along. Our Spirit Section will explore ways you can get in touch with yourself and build your relationship with the world around you.
