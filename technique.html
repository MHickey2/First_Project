Body

How can we help our Bodies

To achieve good physical wellbeing there are a number of key factors that will playa major role. Diet is important, keep to a well balanced diet, curb snacking and ensure you intake key nutrients. Exercise can be a great way to keep in shape, and can suit you regardless of your fitness level. Rest is important and can be built into your day quite easily. Ensure you get enough sleep by establishing a good night time routine. In regards to alcohol limit your consumption and if you smoke, try to cut down or even give up the smoking habit.

Ways to improve your health by working with your Body

  • You don't need to use a Gym, but find something you enjoy and it will be easier to continue.
  • Exercise outside and you can experience nature at the same time
  • Change your diet, add healthy choices, skip the fast food and snack sensibly.
  • Go to bed earlier, ensure you get undisturbed sleep, skip the late night movie.
  • You can drink, but try the sensible approach, know your limit and try no alcohol days.
  • Ensure you get the nutrients, eat a varied diet that covers the major food groups.
  • Don't skip meals, try to keep to breakfast, lunch and dinner and maybe 2 snacks.
  • Try meat free days and make simple changes like low sugar and salt products.

Mind

How can we help our Minds

To achieve good mental wellbeing there are a number of ways you can improve your mood. It is important to connect with others, with the recent pandemic it was hard to keep our social connections strong, so this may be daunting, but even small steps can be beneficial. Ensure you set time aside for relaxation, taking a time out at times of stress can help you get through any crisis. If you need help, reach out to others, its important to maintain good communication and foster good relationships as these will be people that will support you in troubled times.

Ways to improve your health by working with your Mind

  • Find activities that will help you connect with other people, it's good for your mental health.
  • Don't let stress get on top of you, develop good coping skills, try CBD.
  • Reach out to others, maybe volunteer your services in the community.
  • If you have a hobby, join a group where you can share your skills.
  • Find new challenges and it will help build up your motivation.
  • There are a variety of audio meditations available to ease stress and improve your mindset.

Spirit

How can we help our Spirit

To achieve good emotional health, there are ways you can improve or maintain good emotional health. Keep on top of stress, develop strategies to deal with the stress in your life. Be aware of triggers in your life that cause negative emotions, find improved ways to deal with them and take actions that will achieve more positive emotions. Do activities that will build your self esteem and strive for a healthy balance. Reflect and connect with others and forgive yourself when things go wrong, everyone makes mistakes.

Ways to improve your health by working with your Spirit

  • Take time to be alone, create a sanctuary where you can get in touch with your feelings.
  • Declutter your space, try to get rid of anything holding you back, it allows freedom.
  • Practise gratitude, be thankful for what you have now, and appreciate the good thinks.
  • You are not your thoughts, you can have a bad day but don't let it get you down for long.
  • Use a journal to get in touch with your thoughts, contemplate your future goals.
  • You could try affirmations to build yourself up and build yourself up.
  • Read Books and watch movies that inspire you and raise your emotions positively.
  • Use guided meditations to rebalance yourself, your chakras or can clear your aura.
